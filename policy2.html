Privacy Commitment for Admin Services

At Ventora, LLC, we are committed to ensuring the highest standards of confidentiality and data protection in every aspect of our remote service delivery. As a virtual support provider, we handle a wide variety of sensitive client materials, including business documents, credentials, financial data, and internal communications. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and protect this information.

1. Information Collection

We collect only the information necessary to perform services effectively, such as:

  • Contact details (name, email, business name)
  • Project documents and reference materials
  • Access credentials (only when specifically required and authorized)
  • Task-related communication history

We do not collect or store credit card or bank information. All payments are processed through secure third-party gateways.

2. Data Handling & Access

All client files are:

  • Stored securely in encrypted cloud services (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox)
  • Accessed only by authorized team members
  • Regularly reviewed and deleted when no longer required

Client information is never shared or sold. Third-party disclosure occurs only:

  • With written permission
  • When required by law or legal order

3. Platform & Compliance

We work with systems that comply with the latest data protection frameworks, including:

  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
  • HIPAA (when applicable to client needs)
  • ISO 27001 (Information Security Management)

If your business requires compliance with specific frameworks, we will accommodate platform preferences and security protocols.

4. Confidentiality Agreements

All services are covered by an implied confidentiality clause. Upon request, we are happy to sign NDAs provided by the client or issue our own.

5. Retention & Deletion

We retain data only for the time necessary to complete service or meet legal/accounting obligations. Upon request, we will:

  • Return all digital content
  • Wipe all backups and local files
  • Provide written confirmation of deletion
